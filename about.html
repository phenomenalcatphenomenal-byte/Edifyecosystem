About Us

VISION98 – DECENTRALIZED INNOVATION FUNDING

Vision98 was founded in 2022 to bridge the gap between grassroots innovators and global capital.

We are a decentralized platform empowering innovators in emerging economies. Our vision is to become the global standard for grassroots innovation funding – transparent, community-owned, and accessible to anyone with a bold idea. Through the V98 token, stakers vote to fund real‑world projects, and every step is recorded on‑chain.

We are building a trustless ecosystem where innovators can submit ideas, receive micro‑grants, and build their legacy. Our community governs the allocation of funds, ensuring that the most impactful projects rise to the top. Join us in unlocking the world’s untapped potential.

Decentralized

No central authority – decisions are made by V98 token holders through on‑chain governance.

Community Governed

Stakeholders vote directly on grant proposals – transparency and inclusion are paramount.

Global Reach

Grassroots innovators from any country can apply – funding has no borders.
