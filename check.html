Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our crypto savings accounts

To start earning interest, simply create an AxiomPlexus account, complete the verification process, deposit your cryptocurrency into your savings wallet, and choose a savings plan. Interest will start accruing immediately after your deposit is confirmed.

For our Flexible Savings plan, there is no minimum deposit requirement. For our Fixed-Term plans, we require a minimum deposit of $100 equivalent for the 90-Day plan and $500 equivalent for the 365-Day plan.

Interest is compounded and paid out daily for Flexible Savings, weekly for the 90-Day Fixed plan, and monthly for the 365-Day Fixed plan. You can track your earnings in real-time through your dashboard.

For Flexible Savings, you can withdraw your funds at any time with no penalties. For Fixed-Term plans, early withdrawals are subject to a penalty equivalent to 30 days of interest for the 90-Day plan and 60 days of interest for the 365-Day plan.

We generate yield through multiple carefully vetted strategies including crypto lending to institutional borrowers, staking on proof-of-stake blockchains, and providing liquidity to decentralized finance (DeFi) protocols. Our risk management team continuously monitors and optimizes these strategies to maximize returns while minimizing risk.
